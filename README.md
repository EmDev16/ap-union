# ap-union
AP Union is a project made for my backend course in which people will be able to interact creatively in a new way.


So for a big summary of the whole social media page that is AP Union; AP from Advanced Placement, and Union from the idea of uniting people based on their ideas, theories, interests and everyday thoughts, not based on their popularity, beauty, body location or any other personal info.
AP Union is a social media platform that connects people in a different and deeper way.
It shows posts from accounts you follow in chronological order, and it has questions from Admin or entered by users that people can click and answer.
This will help people discuss real topics, give their peace on certain topics, help people gain knowledge from others or just know more about stuff in their own circle.
People with similar interests or ideas, or conflicting ones even, can meet new people on the explore page.
Only three pieces of the content from the user of their choosing, be it writing filmed or pictured, and the profile picture and name of the user with a brief about me.
This will force people to connect with each other based on their ideas and genuine interests.
This is all the fight the futility of current social media platforms, and make people connect with each other in a different and deeper way.



Right sidebar: Has questions from Admin, or entered questions from users. People can click and answer those questions and everyday one or more of those question will be visible for everyone to see.
This will help people discuss real topics, give their peace on certain topics, help people gain knowledge from others or just know more about stuff in your own circle. People with similar interests or ideas, or conflicting ones even, can meet new people on the explore page.
That's why there's no showing of location, or any other personal info. Just the content of the post and the profile picture and name of the user. This will help people connect with each other based on their ideas and interests, not based on their location or any other personal info. This is all the fight the futility of current social media platforms, and make people connect with each other in a different and deeper way.
